# Nawathen

**Smart property management for modern landlords and tenants.**

Nawathen is an all-in-one property management platform that helps property
owners, managers, and tenants handle everything in one place — from listings
and lease agreements to rent collection and maintenance requests.

### What we do
- 🏠 **Property & unit management** — organize your portfolio in one dashboard
- 📄 **Digital leasing** — create, sign, and store lease agreements online
- 💳 **Rent collection** — automated payments, reminders, and tracking
- 🔧 **Maintenance requests** — tenants report issues, managers resolve them faster
- 📊 **Reporting & insights** — occupancy, revenue, and performance at a glance
